Our Vision

One year ago students received emails impersonating various faculty staff. Unfortunately there were confirmed cases of students falling victim to these attacks. Scammers have been evolving their tactics using compromised university emails. That's why we came up with a solution: creating POW. We designed the POW extension with the user in mind. It's purpose being to quiet down that voice in the back of your head that says "this email is fishy". POW scans your emails and makes sure you don't fall for the bait. Since we value our user's privacy, POW only scans when prompted and it does not save any user data.
